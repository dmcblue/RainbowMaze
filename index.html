RainbowMaze:

Your goal is to move from the white space to the black space. Each time you move to a colored space, you will be transported to the nearest space of the same color. If there are multiple spaces at the same distance, one is picked at random. Distance is calculated vertically or horizontally; diagonal distances are thus measured by their longest vertical or horizontal value, not Euclidean distance.

Use the Up, Down, Right, Left keys to play (make sure you have clicked the page first).
